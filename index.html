Background

The Dartmouth Association of Latino Alumni (DALA) is dedicated to serving Dartmouth College’s Latinx Alumni by:

Connecting people with alumni in their area.

Providing them with professional resources.

Keeping them connected to their alma mater.

User Research

I began by conducting 3 interviews with users to explore potential pain points.
I discovered that users felt the website was unreliable, unappealing, and confusing due to its outdated user interface and lack of Call To Actions.

I separated the interview data into categories.
